About Dungoneery


It all began after FokaStudio got Minecraft: Dungones

FokaStudio (former Owner, Lead Developer, Lead Artists, Brainstormer, etc.) was also obsessed with RPG servers. Wynncraft, or some terrible Minehut RPGs that have completely no plot, meaning other than cash grab and their progression was like 58 hours long... They would do just fine!

And then, after turning on MCD just once, his mind was already flooded with ideas...


Oh, sorry! Might have turned this up to 11 accidentaly...

So yeah, Dungoneery is basically a mix between Minecraft: Dungeons and Minercaft: Java Edition (with partial Bedrock support)
